<h1> MarketShop</h1>


<h2> Introduction</h2>
<p> MarketStore is your outlet to give a new life to items you don't want anymore or find items you never knew you need(or in some cases, it'll be exactly what you needed).  </p>

<h2> Technologies Used </h2>
<ul>
    <li> Python</li>
    <li> Django</li>
    <li> PostgreSQL</li>
    <li> Heroku</li>
    <li> Materialize</li>
    <li> JavaScript</li>
    <li> Google Fonts</li>
</ul>
